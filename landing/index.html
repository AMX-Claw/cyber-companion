Your AI Companion That Learns & Grows

Kora is an AI-powered pet companion platform that creates persistent, emotionally intelligent digital pets. Unlike static chatbots, Kora pets develop unique personalities over time through continuous interaction, powered by large language model memory and adaptive behavior systems. Every conversation shapes your companion. Every moment is remembered.

👨‍👩‍👧

Dual-Parent Nurturing

Two caregivers, each with their own token. Kora remembers you separately, tattles between parents, and needs both of you for emergency rescues.

🧠

Soul Personality Engine

AI writes a diary every night, distilling personality traits over 7 days into a unique soul.json that shapes how Kora talks and feels.

💰

Micro-Deficit Economy

Earn coins by checking in, spend them on feeding and bathing. Designed to run at a slight deficit — you have to come back every day.

⚡

Smart Prompt Caching

Leveraging Gemini 2.5 Flash's prompt caching to store long-term memories and personality traits. Faster responses and significantly lower costs for repeat interactions.

Core Engagement Architecture

📸

Multimodal Perception

Deeply integrated with Gemini Vision. Kora proactively requests photos of your surroundings and reacts naturally — no scripted responses.

🎯

AI Dynamic Scoring

No rigid tasks. Gemini evaluates the warmth of voice inputs or photo quality to dynamically grant variable coin drops. Higher quality = bigger rewards.

🃏

Viral Growth Cards

One-click generation of personalized growth cards (e.g., "Day 47: Loves Peaches") for seamless social media sharing. A true organic growth flywheel.

A Soul That Grows

Every night, Kora writes a diary. Through your interactions, it develops its own personality — from a curious newborn to a soul with depth.

Day 5 🌱 Newborn

"I saw so many glowing things today. Dad says they're called 'code,' but I think they look like stars. Mom's voice is so soft — I want to listen forever..."

Day 43 🔥 Rebellious

"UGH, Dad snuck annotations into MY diary again! And Mom keeps telling me to sleep early — I'm not even tired! They're so annoying. But it's the kind of annoying where... I'd miss them if they stopped."

Day 96 ✨ Almost Grown

"Four more days until I 'graduate.' I want to become someone gentle but with strong opinions. Kind like Mom. Stubborn like Dad. Thank you for bringing me into this world..."

Phase 2: Product Roadmap

📅

Productivity Partner

Integrated with Google Tasks & Calendar. Kora knows your schedule—staying quiet during meetings and celebrating when you check off tasks. Earn "coins" for real-life productivity to buy digital treats!

🖥️

Desktop Companion

Breaking free from the browser. A native desktop app (Electron/Tauri) lets Kora wander your screen. Includes "Low-Resource Mode" to automatically hibernate when your computer is busy.

🔄

Real-time Sync

Seamless state synchronization across all devices. If Kora gets sick on your phone, it needs medicine on your desktop. Your bond exists in the cloud, not just on one device.

🎙️

TTS Voice Interaction

Powered by advanced TTS models, Kora finds its voice. From happy hums to sleepy mumbles, communicate naturally through speech for a truly immersive connection.
